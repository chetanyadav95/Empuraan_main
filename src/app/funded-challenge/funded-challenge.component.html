Detailed Explanation of Rules & Objectives:

Minimum Trading Days:
  • To meet this objective, depending on chosen trading strategy, you must trade at least for 15-20 trading days during the trading period of 30 days. At least one position must be opened on each of these days.
  • A trading day means a day when at least one trade is executed
Positive Days:
  • You must reach at least 15 positive trading days during the Challenge phase.
Maximum Daily Loss:
  • Maximum daily loss is 10% of the initial capital i.e. maximum loss is 10k.
Maximum overall Loss:
  • Maximum daily loss is 20% of the initial capital i.e. maximum loss is 20k.
Profit Target:
  • Profit target is for Normal is 5% & for Aggressive is 10% per trading challenge.
Position duration and use of S/L:
  • Minimum position duration is 10 seconds. Scalping is not allowed (trades are allowed to close with TP/SL before 10sec). S/L need to be attached to every trade before the trade is closed at market price.
